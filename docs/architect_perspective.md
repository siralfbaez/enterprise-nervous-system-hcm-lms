# 🧠 Architect’s Perspective: The "Nervous System" Strategy

## The Problem: The "Data Lag" in Enterprise AI

Most enterprises approach AI integrations with a "point-to-point" mindset—polling an HCM like Workday or an LMS like Cornerstone every few hours. This creates a **stale data problem**.

If a New Hire starts at 9:00 AM, but the AI doesn't see them until 2:00 PM, the "First 30 Minutes" engagement window—the most critical time for onboarding—is already lost.
